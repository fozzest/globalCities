Essence

Where we are

What binds us together? What is a chair always a chair? Why is a seat round and comforting? Why can’t we escape these fundamental parts fo our life? Why should we maybe not try to escape, but to learn, embrace, and grow?

Where we are going

We are a population of primates that is capable of doing and making incredible things. Yet as time moves forward, our past becomes critiqued, distorted, even lost. History is extremely fragile. In our contemporary society where Cancel culture is rampant, what are the implications in design?
